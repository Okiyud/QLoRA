# Commercial performance

The Mountain debuted at number-one the U.K.’s Official Albums Chart and joins the band’s 2005 album Demon Days and 2023’s Cracker Island as chart-topping albums, The Official Charts Company reports that with its 30,000 units in the opening week, The Mountain is the biggest independent release of 2026 so far.[89]

The Mountain debuted at number seven on the US Billboard 200 with first-week sales of 53,000 equivalent album units, which includes 38,000 in pure album sales and 15,000 streaming units. It became the group's sixth top-ten record in the United States.[90]
