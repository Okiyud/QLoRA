# The Hardest Thing

Featuring:
Tony Allen

The Hardest Thing (and by extension The Mountain) explores themes of loss and heartbreak.

The intro to the song features Tony Allen (long time friend and collaborator of Gorillaz co-creator Damon Albarn) who passed away in 2020.

The blow was so massive. He was one of my favorite people on earth. He felt like family, really, really profoundly. All my family loved him as well. He was beloved, beloved. Truly a beloved man.
- Damon Albarn
