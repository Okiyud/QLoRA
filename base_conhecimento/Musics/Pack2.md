# Casablanca

Featuring:
Paul Simonon & Johnny Marr.

“Casablanca” is the 13th track on The Mountain, featuring Paul Simonon and Johnny Marr. The song is named after the 1942 film of the same name, and its famous city in Morocco.

# The Sweet Prince

Featuring:
Ajay Prasanna, Johnny Marr & Anoushka Shankar

The Sweet Prince depicts the final moments of Damon Albarn’s father, Keith Albarn, who passed away in 2024 during the creation of the album.

The track takes inspiration from Indian cultures depicture of death, suggesting death is not the end, only a step into another life.

# The Sad God

Featuring:
Black Thought, Ajay Prasanna & Anoushka Shankar.

The Sad God is the final track of the standard edition of Gorillaz’s ninth studio album, The Mountain. It uses motifs from the intro song and namesake of the album, The Mountain.

The song depicts a god overlooking the Earth and humans, sharing it’s disappointment with our actions.
