# The Moon Cave

Featuring
Asha Puthli, Bobby Womack, Trugoy the Dove, Jalen Ngonda & Black Thought.

“The Moon Cave” is the second track and was the former-planned 3rd single from Gorillaz' ninth studio album, “The Mountain”.

This track was formerly registered on ASCAP official website on October 30, 2025, leading to people safely assuming that this track was the next single for “The Mountain”.

Albarn begins the journey up the mountain the Moon Cave, a place where he hides his feelings, thoughts and memories. He spends his time here reflecting on his life, and wondering how he ended up where he has.

Notably the track posthumously features Dave Jolicoeur, long time Gorillaz collaborator and one third of legendary rap trio De Le Soul.

The recordings of Dave’s voice come from an unreleased Gorillaz track called “Float Tropics” featuring De La Soul recorded during sessions for 2010s “Plastic Beach”.
