# The God of Lying

Featuring:
IDLES.

“The God of Lying” is the third single from Gorillaz’s ninth studio album, The Mountain.

A snippet appeared on the Kong Studios website prior to release, before the song premiered on BBC Radio 1 on November 6, 2025.

It replaced “The Moon Cave” as the planned third single.

# The Empty Dream Machine

Featuring:
Black Thought, Johnny Marr & Anoushka Shankar.

The Empty Dream Machine by Gorillaz is the 7th track from their ninth studio album, The Mountain (2026), It explores themes of grief, spiritual search, like much of the album and this song specifically, is about recovery from a heavy loss.

# Delirium

Featuring
Mark E. Smith.

When an outside force with great power arrives bringing change, terror and destruction, Gorillaz call for action and change.

The chorus has a posthumous feature from Mark E Smith, who passed away in 2018.

The recordings of Mark featured on Delirium are taken from recording sessions from Gorillaz 2010 album “Plastic Beach”, where Smith featured on the track “Glitter Freeze”.
