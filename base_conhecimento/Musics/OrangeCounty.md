# Orange County

Featuring
Bizarrap, Kara Jackson & Anoushka Shankar.

After completing “The Hardest Thing” (music from the album) the band felt there might be more ideas worth exploring with the tune, later doing so while in Orange County.

While The Mountain and The Hardest Thing explore themes of grief and death, Gorillaz co-creators Damon Albarn and Jamie Hewlett clarify they feel “Orange County” is more about a breakup or lost love.

In 2023 Damon Albarn’s relationship with his partner of 25 years ended.

“Orange County can also be about losing someone you love, not through death, but through seperation.” “In the second part that’s more what it’s about, it becomes a duet.”
- Jamie Hewlett & Damon Albarn

The song "Orange County" features guest contributions from Anoushka Shankar and Kara Jackson, as well as additional production from Bizarrap, who also produced an alternative mix of the song.[33] Albarn was introduced to Jackson's music through Little Simz, who the band had previously worked with on the Humanz B-side "Garage Palace".[34] Albarn had originally planned for the song to feature Mexican singer and rapper Peso Pluma, and even travelled to his recording studio in Orange County. Although the collaboration ultimately did not come to fruition, it inspired the song's title.[35]
